FAQ

Questions

Do I need to give you my AWS credentials?

No. MarketReady works with your publicly visible Marketplace listing URL, or you can manually enter your listing details. We never ask for AWS access keys.

How is the GTM Score calculated?

We evaluate 18 criteria across 5 GTM pillars: listing quality, discoverability, pricing fit, co-sell readiness, and buyer trust signals. Each criterion is weighted by its revenue impact based on data from top-performing Marketplace listings.

I'm not listed on Marketplace yet. Is this useful?

Absolutely. Use the ICP Builder and Pricing Advisor before you submit your listing. Get it right the first time instead of fixing it after launch.

Can I use this for multiple listings or clients?

The Agency plan supports up to 15 client workspaces with white-label PDF reports. Pro supports unlimited audits for a single company or product.

Is there a money-back guarantee?

Yes. If you don't find at least 3 actionable improvements to your listing in your first audit, we'll refund your first month. No questions asked.
